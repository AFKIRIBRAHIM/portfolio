About me

A little about me

My journey in IT began at the Specialized Institute of Applied Technology, where I studied computer networks and earned a diploma as a Specialized Technician. The program focused mostly on hardware, but ever since I was young, I've been more fascinated by software — how websites, apps, and games are built.

That passion led me to continue my studies in Applied Computer Science at Thomas More University, which offers a strong, future-oriented foundation including modern fields like artificial intelligence. I'm very happy with that decision, as I can clearly see my growth both technically and personally.

After earning my bachelor's degree, I aim to work as a developer, applying the knowledge and skills I gained at Thomas More in a real-world company setting — while also planning to build my own tech startup.

Outside of tech, I enjoy swimming and horseback riding. One of my more unique hobbies is collecting old-timer cars — something I love doing in my free time.
